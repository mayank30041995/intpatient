Frequently asked questions

Who will I speak with on WhatsApp?

A trained care coordinator will guide you to the appropriate specialty team and help with next steps.

Do I need reports to start?

No. You can start with a brief description. Reports help with precision but are optional initially.

Is my information confidential?

Yes. Your information is used only for guidance and coordination support.

Will I be pressured to book treatment?

No. We provide clarity and support; you decide whether and when to proceed.

Can I take an online opinion first?

Yes. Many patients start with guidance/medical opinion and decide about travel later.
