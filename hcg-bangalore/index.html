Overview

HealthCare Global Enterprises Ltd.(HCG Hospitals), founded by Dr. BS Ajaikumar is focused on cancer care. HCG's primary focus is on cancer care, advanced screening and diagnostics. Its headquarter is in Bangalore, a Metro city in South India. HCG is a combined pool of 450 + specialists across various domains. HCG centers are carefully arrayed to facilitate the highest quality clinical care for patients from all walks of life. HCG's operations are spread across 20 cities in India by its expansion into Tier II and III cities, towns, and villages.HCG has also set up a comprehensive cancer center in Nairobi, Kenya.
