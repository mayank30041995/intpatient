About Treatment Traveller

About us

At Treatment Traveller, we are here to help you reach out to the best in healthcare.Providing an ideal platform which comprises of doctors, healthcare specialists and communication professionals. Thereby, aiding the search of patients towards receiving treatment in the prestigious hospitals in India.

Based in Delhi NCR Region, the collaboration of our associates from all over India is at the heart of our commitment to assist overseas patients and provide them with home-like caring atmosphere throughout their treatment while in hospital. On top of it, we also make continuous efforts to help patients receive considerably lower costs and almost zero waiting periods. In a short span of time, we have earned the love and accolades from a large number of visiting patients from all parts of the world.
