A specialized healthcare facility focused on nephrology, urology, and renal transplants. Known for its expertise in advanced kidney care and minimally invasive urological procedures.

  • • Specialized in kidney care, nephrology, and urology
  • • Extensive experience in renal transplant procedures
  • • Best suited for kidney-related treatments

International Patient Services

Fortis’ international patient support is built around coordinated appointments and practical travel support.

Appointment coordination

An international patient coordinator liaises with the hospital and arranges appointments, referrals, lab/imaging and inpatient procedure routing.

Stay & accommodation

Accommodation can be arranged near the hospital based on budget, including guest houses, service apartments and hotels.

Airport & arrival support

Airport pickup/drop and direct escort to hospital or accommodation can be arranged before arrival.

Visa / FRRO / interpreter help

Support includes visa requisition guidance, FRRO assistance, language interpreters and practical patient services.
