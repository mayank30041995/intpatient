Frequently Asked Questions

Who will I speak with on WhatsApp or Live Chat?

You will speak with a trained care coordinator who helps international patients connect with the appropriate Apollo Delhi specialty team and understand next steps.

Do I need medical reports to start?

No. You can start with a brief description of your condition. Reports help with more precise guidance but are optional initially.

Is my information confidential?

Yes. Your details are handled confidentially and used only to guide and coordinate your request.

Will I be pressured to book treatment?

No. Our role is to provide clarity and support. You decide whether to proceed and when.

Can I get guidance before traveling to India?

Yes. Many international patients begin with guidance/medical opinion first and travel only if they decide it is beneficial.
