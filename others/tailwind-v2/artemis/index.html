About Artemis Hospitals

Artemis Health Institute was established in 2007 and is a healthcare venture launched by the Apollo Tyres Group promoters. Artemis was the first hospital in Gurgaon to get Joint Commission International (J.C.I.) accreditation in 2013 and also the first Hospital in Haryana to get NABH accreditation within 3 years of start-up It is designed as one of the exceptional hospitals in India, providing expertise in advanced medical & surgical interventions in inpatient and outpatient services. Artemis has provided modern technology in the hands of reputed doctors from all over the country and overseas to set a new benchmark in healthcare.
