Fortis in Kolkata: both branches on one page

Choose between the two Fortis units in Kolkata depending on the kind of care you need.

Request appointment

Branch 1

Fortis Hospital, Anandapur

Multispecialty tertiary-care hospital in Kolkata with 230+ operational beds, 10 OTs and 72 ICU beds. Strong visibility in cardiac sciences, gastroenterology, pulmonology, neurosciences, orthopaedics and robotic surgery.

  • • NABH and NABL accredited
  • • Robotic surgery and advanced GI / cardiac / neuro capabilities
  • • Better suited when broad multispecialty support is required

Branch 2

Fortis Hospital & Kidney Institute, Gariahat

A 60-bedded specialized facility known in Eastern India for renal care, urology and nephrology. It introduced several advanced renal/urology procedures early in Kolkata and has performed hundreds of kidney transplants.

  • • Specialized renal, nephrology and urology focus
  • • Pioneer in advanced renal procedures in Eastern India
  • • Better suited when kidney/urology pathways are the main need

International Patient Services

Fortis’ international patient support is built around coordinated appointments and practical travel support.

Appointment coordination

An international patient coordinator liaises with the hospital and arranges appointments, referrals, lab/imaging and inpatient procedure routing.

Stay & accommodation

Accommodation can be arranged near the hospital based on budget, including guest houses, service apartments and hotels.

Airport & arrival support

Airport pickup/drop and direct escort to hospital or accommodation can be arranged before arrival.

Visa / FRRO / interpreter help

Support includes visa requisition guidance, FRRO assistance, language interpreters and practical patient services.
