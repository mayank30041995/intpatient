Infrastructure

Sanjeevani Kaya Shodhan Sansthan is thoughtfully designed to support natural healing, comfort, and inner calm through a perfect blend of nature and modern wellness facilities.

Serene & Nature-Rich Campus

  • Green Healing Environment: Lush greenery with open spaces that promote calmness and recovery.
  • Pollution-Free Campus: Noise-free surroundings supporting mental peace.
  • Healing Gardens & Walkways: Designed for gentle movement and mindfulness.
  • Natural Light & Fresh Air: Architecture that enhances circulation and vitality.

Clinical & Therapy Infrastructure

  • Naturopathy Treatment Units: Dedicated spaces for detox and natural therapies.
  • Hydrotherapy & Bath Therapy Section: Fully equipped water-based healing units.
  • Massage & Physical Therapy Rooms: Designed for comfort and therapeutic effectiveness.
  • Ayurveda & Panchakarma Units: Traditional setups following authentic protocols.
  • Acupressure & Physiotherapy Areas: Focused on pain relief and functional recovery.
  • Medical Consultation Rooms: Hygienic and private assessment spaces.

Yoga & Meditation Facilities

  • Spacious Yoga Halls: Well-ventilated areas for group and individual practice.
  • Meditation & Pranayama Zones: Quiet spaces for inner balance.
  • Natural Flooring & Ambience: Enhancing grounding and focus.
  • Dedicated Practice Areas: Separate zones for therapy-based sessions.

Residential & Stay Facilities

  • Comfortable Accommodation: Clean and well-maintained rooms.
  • Flexible Stay Options: Single and shared rooms available.
  • Peaceful Living Environment: Supports deep healing and rest.
  • Recovery-Focused Design: Rooms designed for relaxation and renewal.

Dining & Nutrition Area

  • Wellness Dining Hall: Dedicated space for mindful eating.
  • Satvik & Therapeutic Meals: Prepared as per treatment protocols.
  • Fresh & Hygienic Food: Naturally cooked with quality ingredients.
  • Personalized Nutrition: Diet plans aligned with individual therapy needs.

Support Facilities

  • 24/7 Utilities: Continuous water and electricity supply.
  • Comfort Amenities: Hot water, geysers, and climate support.
  • Housekeeping & Laundry: Regular cleaning and maintenance.
  • Secure Parking: Safe vehicle parking within campus.
  • High-Speed Wi-Fi: Available for essential communication.
  • Safety Lockers: Secure storage for personal belongings.
