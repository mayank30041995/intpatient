About Sanjeevani Kaya Shodhan Sansthan

Sanjeevani Kaya Shodhan Sansthan is a holistic healthcare and wellness institute offering an integrated approach of Naturopathy, Ayurveda, Yoga, and Allied Therapies. Designed for people seeking long-term relief from chronic and lifestyle-related conditions, Sanjeevani focuses on root-cause healing, detoxification, and life transformation in a calm, nature-rich environment.

Located in Gohana, Sonipat (Haryana), Sanjeevani provides a peaceful retreat away from city stress, where healing begins naturally.
