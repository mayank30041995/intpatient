About KIMS Health Trivandrum

KIMSHEALTH is one of the leading hospitals in Trivandrum, Kerala. Established in 2002 as a 250-bed quaternary care center in Thiruvananthapuram, it has grown into one of India’s largest and most trusted healthcare networks.

Managed and operated by KIMS Health Care Management Limited, a public limited company incorporated in 1999, KIMSHEALTH was founded with a strong vision to make world-class healthcare accessible and affordable for everyone.

Accreditations & Quality Standards

KIMSHEALTH has played a pioneering role in advancing quality standards in Indian healthcare. It was the first hospital in the country to achieve both NABH and ACHSI accreditations. Its laboratories and blood center are accredited by NABL and NABH, reflecting adherence to international benchmarks.

International Patient Services

The International Patient Relations Department ensures a seamless experience for overseas patients, offering visa assistance, language translation services, and international cuisine options for personalized care.
