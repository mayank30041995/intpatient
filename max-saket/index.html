Hospital overview

World Class Clinical Outcomes in the Heart of New Delhi

Max Super Speciality Hospital, Saket is one of the best-known hospitals in the country and a major South Delhi flagship with 539+ beds. It has a complete spectrum of diagnostic and therapeutic technologies, including 450+ doctors, 38+ specialities, 920+ trained staff, and more than 80,000 surgeries done.

The care journey is framed around appointment bookings, doctor consultations, second opinions, estimates, and structured support for overseas patients. This makes Saket especially suited to a high-conversion wireframe that emphasizes fast action and high clinical confidence.
