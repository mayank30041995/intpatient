About Meitra Hospital, Calicut – Advanced Quaternary Care

Established in 2012, Meitra Hospital was founded by a group of visionary healthcare professionals with the goal of building a world-class quaternary care institution in India. The hospital was created with a mission to deliver high-quality healthcare services across all sections of society while ensuring an exceptional patient experience.

Located in Calicut, Meitra is an advanced quaternary care hospital built to international standards. Its infrastructure, clinical services, and medical facilities are aligned with global best practices. Spread across more than 450,000 square feet, the hospital houses 220 individual patient rooms and suites, 8 fully equipped operating theatres, and 52 dedicated intensive care units, supported by advanced diagnostic technology.

Patient recovery at Meitra is enhanced through precise clinical treatment combined with a thoughtfully designed healing environment. The hospital has been recognized for excellence in design and architecture and is accredited by NABH and Green OT, reflecting its commitment to quality, safety, and sustainable healthcare practices.

Meitra delivers specialized patient care through integrated practice models known as Centres of Excellence (COEs). Each centre brings together medical and surgical specialties focused on specific disease areas or organ systems under a unified leadership structure. The hospital offers COEs in Heart and Vascular Care, Bone, Joint and Spine Care, Neurosciences, Gastro Sciences, Nephro-Urosciences, Blood Diseases, Bone Marrow Transplant, and Cancer Immunotherapy.

These Centres of Excellence are supported by experienced specialists, modern medical infrastructure, and technology-driven clinical protocols. Developed with guidance from physicians associated with one of the world’s leading hospitals, Meitra’s structured care-path approach ensures consistently high clinical outcomes and distinguishes it within the healthcare sector.

At Meitra Hospital, patients remain at the core of every service. The hospital is committed to serving the community through ethical, transparent, and patient-centric healthcare practices, striving to elevate healthcare standards and positively transform patient experiences across India.
