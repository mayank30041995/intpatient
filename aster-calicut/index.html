Hospital overview

A clearer hospital view for international patients

Aster MIMS Calicut presents itself as one of the leading healthcare systems in Malabar, offering comprehensive healthcare services with global standards. The hospital describes itself as a 600-bedded multispecialty centre renowned for excellent medical expertise, nursing care, and quality diagnostic services.

Its international-patients messaging focuses on bringing patients closer to the healthcare they need, with a care model built around specialised doctors, technology-enabled treatment, and patient-centric delivery. The hospital also highlights its NABH and NABL accreditations as part of its quality framework and stresses a broad service portfolio spanning primary, secondary, tertiary, and quaternary care.
