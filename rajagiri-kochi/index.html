Hospital overview

Get Cared For by the best

Rajagiri Hospital clearly positions its International Patient Care Helpdesk as a single-window service for patients travelling from abroad. Their helpdesk supports patients from registration to discharge while helping them move seamlessly across departments with strict infection control practices.

To support informed decision-making, Rajagiri offers preliminary treatment guidance based on medical reports shared in advance, helping patients plan their travel and care journey with greater clarity and confidence. Recognized as a trusted referral center, the hospital delivers advanced medical care across key specialties including oncology, orthopedics, cardiac sciences, gastroenterology, hematology, and spine surgery.
