Overview

Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital is the only Hospital with a Full-Time Specialist System (FTSS), which ensures easy availability and accessibility to specialists exclusive to Hospital. Kokilaben Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital uses Protocol and Care Pathway based treatment models to provide the best outcomes for patients. Our FTSS team is committed in providing first-class healthcare to every patient. The entire Specialist Team keeps up with a Common Plan so that no time is wasted in getting expert advice for the simplest clinical problems or the most complex disorders. Our system makes sure the best outcomes for our patients with state-of-the-art infrastructure, cutting-edge technology, and Full-Time Specialists. The model guarantees excellence in delivering care to very critical patients after complex interventions or surgeries, leading to success rates matching that of the best centers in our country and overseas. Based on the practice prevalent in premier international centers, we aim to deliver subspecialty-based care in major clinical areas. This approach is unique among private medical centers in the country.
